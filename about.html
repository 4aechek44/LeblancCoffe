The Story of Leblanc

Leblanc is a small coffee shop located in the quiet residential district of Ёнген-Джая. Over the years, it has become known as a place where simplicity, quality, and a calm atmosphere come together.

· · ·

At first glance, Leblanc may seem modest and unassuming. However, its charm lies precisely in that simplicity. The shop was founded by Sojiro Sakura, a man known for his attention to detail and deep appreciation for the art of coffee making.

His philosophy focuses on respecting traditional brewing methods while creating a space where every guest feels welcome.

As time passed, Leblanc grew into more than just a coffee shop. It became a familiar part of the neighborhood — a place where people come to take a break from the rush of daily life, enjoy a quiet moment, or have a relaxed conversation.

The interior features warm tones, wooden elements, and soft lighting, all contributing to a cozy and intimate environment. The second floor, in particular, offers a more private setting, ideal for working, studying, or simply spending time alone.

Despite its small size, Leblanc has built a reputation as a place with character. It is not only about the coffee, but about the atmosphere — calm, genuine, and slightly removed from the fast pace of the city.

Today, Leblanc remains a symbol of comfort and simplicity,
attracting those who are looking for more than just a cup of coffee.
